The Challenge

Digital Transformation for Member-First Banking

One of Texas's largest credit unions—serving over 350,000 members—faced a critical inflection point. Member expectations had shifted toward digital-first experiences, but the organization's digital ecosystem had evolved organically, creating fragmented experiences that didn't reflect their commitment to service excellence.

Fragmented Experience

Years of organic growth had created inconsistent touchpoints across digital and physical channels. Members experienced different brands depending on how they interacted.

Shifting Expectations

Members increasingly expected digital-first experiences that matched what they received from fintech apps—without losing the personal touch credit unions are known for.

Data Blind Spots

Despite serving 350,000+ members, TDECU lacked systematic insight into member needs, preferences, and pain points across their journey.

Our Approach

Research-Driven Member Understanding

We built a comprehensive research program that gave TDECU unprecedented visibility into member needs—then translated those insights into strategic action.

Member Channel Research

Comprehensive analysis across digital and physical touchpoints. Understanding how members actually navigate the credit union—not how we assume they do.

Brand Perception Studies

Validating positioning with target demographics. Ensuring TDECU's brand promise resonates with current and prospective members.

Creative Asset Testing

Ensuring marketing and communication resonate with members before launch. Reducing waste on campaigns that don't land.

Usability Testing

Continuous evaluation of digital experiences. Identifying friction points and opportunities for improvement across the member journey.

The Work

Strategic Design Partnership

Website Strategy

Aligning digital presence with brand promise. Creating a website experience that reflects TDECU's member-first values while enabling efficient self-service.

Channel Optimization

Data-driven improvements based on member behavior insights. Understanding how members prefer to interact—and designing experiences that meet them where they are.

Ongoing Partnership

Embedded team model providing continuous UX support. We're not a vendor—we're an extension of TDECU's digital team.

Digital Transformation

Supporting TDECU's evolution from traditional credit union to digital-first financial partner—without losing the human touch that members value.
