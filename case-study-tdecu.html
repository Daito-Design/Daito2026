The Challenge

Digital Transformation for Member-First Banking

One of Texas's largest credit unions, serving over 350,000 members, faced a critical inflection point. Member expectations had shifted toward digital-first experiences, but the organization's digital ecosystem had evolved organically, creating fragmented experiences that didn't reflect their commitment to service excellence.

Operational Bottlenecks

Years of organic growth had created friction points across digital and physical channels. Long click paths and fragmented workflows were slowing member journeys and increasing support costs.

Strategic Decision Gaps

Executive leadership needed data-driven insights to guide go-to-market strategy, brand positioning, and digital investment decisions—not just opinions and assumptions.

Untapped Member Intelligence

Despite serving 350,000+ members, TDECU lacked systematic research programs to uncover bottlenecks, validate brand positioning, and inform strategic planning at the highest levels.

Our Approach

Research-Driven Member Understanding

We built a comprehensive research program that gave TDECU unprecedented visibility into member needs, then translated those insights into strategic action.

Click Path Optimization

Systematic analysis of member journeys to identify and eliminate friction. Reduced click paths by 40% across critical workflows like account opening and loan applications.

Bottleneck Identification

Deep operational insights revealing where member journeys break down. Data-driven recommendations that remove friction points and improve conversion at every stage.

Brand Validation Testing

Large-scale market research validating brand positioning with target demographics. Ensuring TDECU's brand promise resonates with current and prospective members.

Executive Decision Support

Research presentations to the highest levels of leadership. Go-to-market strategic planning informed by member insights, not assumptions.

The Work

Strategic Design Partnership

Website Strategy

Aligning digital presence with brand promise. Creating a website experience that reflects TDECU's member-first values while enabling efficient self-service.

Channel Optimization

Data-driven improvements based on member behavior insights. Understanding how members prefer to interact, and designing experiences that meet them where they are.

Ongoing Partnership

Embedded team model providing continuous UX support. We're not a vendor. We're an extension of TDECU's digital team.

Digital Transformation

Supporting TDECU's evolution from traditional credit union to digital-first financial partner, without losing the human touch that members value.
