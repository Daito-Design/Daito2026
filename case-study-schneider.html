The Challenge

Visualizing the Art of the Possible

Schneider Electric needed to showcase 6 AI-driven concepts at ADIPEC 2025—not just explain existing technology, but paint a compelling vision of autonomous operations and what the future could look like.

6 AI Concepts

Six distinct AI-driven visions for autonomous operations, each requiring unique visual storytelling to communicate possibility without overpromising.

Future Visualization

Showing the "art of the possible"—not just current capabilities, but where AI-powered energy management is heading. Inspiring without being speculative.

ADIPEC Presence

Standing out at the world's largest energy conference. Creating visual experiences that position Schneider as the leader in AI-driven autonomous operations.

Design System

A Visual Language for Autonomous Intelligence

We created a consistent design language combining dark spatial layers, neon accents, and glass-like depth. This approach gave Schneider's AI presentations a futuristic but credible tone.

Color Palette

Dark spatial base with Schneider green accents and cyan highlights for data visualization.

Typography

Inter Bold
Clean, technical, highly legible at any size. Perfect for data-dense presentations.
