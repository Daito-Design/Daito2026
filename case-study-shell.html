The Challenge

Enterprise Complexity at Global Scale

One of the world's largest energy companies was digitally transforming how their geoscientists work. With exploration teams spanning six continents, thousands of specialist users, and decades of legacy systems, they needed a partner who could navigate enterprise complexity while delivering intuitive, powerful tools.

Billion-Dollar Decisions

These applications inform drilling decisions worth hundreds of millions. Usability gaps don't just frustrate users—they can lead to costly mistakes in geological interpretation.

Legacy System Debt

Decades of accumulated technical decisions had created a fragmented ecosystem. New tools needed to integrate seamlessly while modernizing the user experience.

Extreme Environments

From offshore platforms with intermittent connectivity to research centers with demanding display requirements—design solutions needed to work everywhere.

Our Approach

Research-Driven Enterprise Design

We embedded ourselves in Shell's world—visiting platforms, shadowing interpreters, and understanding the nuances of geoscience workflows before designing a single screen.

The Work

A Portfolio of Exploration Tools

Since 2018, we've designed and refined multiple applications—each addressing different aspects of the exploration workflow.

Exploration App 1

Subsurface data interpretation tools for geoscientists. Complex visualization of seismic, well log, and geological model data.

Exploration App 2

Collaborative interpretation workspace. Real-time multi-user editing with version control and annotation systems.

Exploration App 3

Production digitization platform. Transitioning field operations from paper-based workflows to digital-first processes.

Mobile Workbench

Field worker mobile solution optimized for offshore environments—gloved use, intermittent connectivity, safety-critical operations.

AI Exploration Agent

Next-generation agentic experience design. Human-AI collaboration for geological interpretation and decision support.

Design System

Unified component library ensuring visual and interaction consistency across the entire exploration tool portfolio.
