The Problem

Agentic AI is being built by engineers,
for engineers.

Most enterprise AI initiatives fail not because the technology doesn't work, but because the humans can't work with it.

AI agents are being deployed without consideration for how operators will monitor them, when they should intervene, or how to build the trust required for effective human-AI collaboration.

Our Approach

Enterprise Human Factors for AI

We apply decades of human factors research from aviation, nuclear, and industrial safety to the design of AI agent systems.

01

Calibrated Trust

Users need to know when to trust the agent and when to intervene. We design transparency mechanisms that build appropriate trust, not blind faith or excessive skepticism.

02

Explainable Actions

Operators can't supervise what they don't understand. We ensure every agent action can be explained in human terms, not technical logs.

03

Graceful Handoff

When agents reach their limits, control must transfer smoothly to humans. We design escalation paths that preserve context and enable rapid human response.

04

Appropriate Autonomy

Not every task should be automated. We help organizations find the right balance between agent autonomy and human oversight for their risk profile.

Traditional AI Implementation

Ship it and hope

  • Technical accuracy as the primary metric
  • Black-box decision making
  • All-or-nothing automation
  • Training as an afterthought
  • Users adapt to the AI
  • Failure = override the system
Daito's AXD Approach

Design for adoption

  • Human-AI team performance as the metric
  • Explainable, auditable reasoning
  • Graduated autonomy with clear boundaries
  • Change management from day one
  • AI adapts to user mental models
  • Failure = graceful degradation + learning

Our Process

How we work

01

Field Research

We embed with your operators to understand their work, their workarounds, and their pain points. No AI strategy should be built without understanding the humans involved.

02

Interaction Design

We design the conversation between human and agent: when to inform, when to ask, when to act. We prototype and test with real users before a single line of AI code is written.

03

Change Enablement

We don't just deliver designs. We help organizations build the muscle memory for human-AI collaboration. Training, rollout strategy, and continuous optimization.
