The Challenge

Making Invisible Technology Tangible

How do you visualize invisible technology? How do you communicate an audio experience through visual media? NTT needed to bring complex bone conduction and spatial audio technology to life for consumers and investors.

Invisible Technology

Bone conduction and spatial audio are inherently invisible. The design challenge was making users feel and understand something they can't see.

Technical Complexity

The engineering behind spatial audio is sophisticated. Communicating this without overwhelming non-technical audiences required careful visual translation.

Consumer Market

Moving from engineering concept to consumer product required understanding listener preferences and mental models around audio.
