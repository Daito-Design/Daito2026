The Challenge

Three Audiences. One Unified Platform.

SunPower needed to transform their solar operations and maintenance capabilities with a comprehensive digital platform. The challenge: create a multi-user mobile ecosystem that serves technicians in the field, managers monitoring portfolios, and customers tracking their solar investments.

Multi-User Complexity

Three distinct user types — field technicians, operations managers, and end customers — each with fundamentally different needs, contexts, and technical literacy.

Field Conditions

Technicians work on rooftops and in the field, often with connectivity challenges. Mobile-first design was non-negotiable.

Real-Time Data

IoT sensor networks, weather data, and predictive maintenance algorithms all needed to feed into a coherent user experience.

Our Approach

How We Approached It

We designed from the field up — starting with technician workflows, then building management and customer layers on the same data foundation.

1

Field Research

On-site observation with technicians during installations and maintenance. Understanding the physical realities of rooftop work.

2

Platform Architecture

Designing a unified data layer that serves three distinct interface paradigms — mobile for techs, dashboard for managers, portal for customers.

3

IoT Integration

Connecting sensor networks, weather APIs, and predictive maintenance models into actionable interface elements.

4

Iterative Testing

Usability testing with all three user groups to validate that each interface served its audience effectively.

The Work

What We Built

We designed and delivered a complete multi-platform ecosystem spanning field operations, portfolio management, and customer engagement.

Technician Mobile App

Field-first mobile application for installation and maintenance workflows. Offline-capable, designed for outdoor use.

Operations Dashboard

Portfolio-wide monitoring and management interface. Real-time performance analytics and work order management.

Customer Portal

Customer-facing monitoring interface showing solar investment performance, savings, and system health.

Predictive Maintenance

Integration of predictive algorithms to optimize maintenance schedules and reduce unnecessary truck rolls.

Results

The Impact

A unified platform that reduced operational friction across all three user types while improving maintenance efficiency.
