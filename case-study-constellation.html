The Challenge

Transforming Safety-Critical Operations

A major nuclear utility operating multiple power plants was digitizing decades of paper-based procedures. The goal: transform static documents into intelligent, interactive digital workflows that guide operators through complex tasks while maintaining the safety margins that nuclear operations demand.

Zero-Error Tolerance

In nuclear environments, every design decision carries weight. The interfaces we create must prevent errors before they happen, not just catch them after.

Regulatory Complexity

NRC and INPO regulations govern every aspect of nuclear operations. Digital systems must maintain compliance while improving efficiency.

Legacy Integration

Eight different systems had evolved over decades. New digital tools needed to consolidate functionality while preserving institutional knowledge.

Our Approach

Error Prevention as a Design Principle

We approached nuclear UX with the humility it demands, listening first, designing second, and treating every interface element as a potential point of failure or protection.

Safety-Critical Design Philosophy

In nuclear operations, the best error is one that never happens. We designed every workflow, every screen, and every interaction to prevent mistakes, not just display warnings when they occur. This means progressive disclosure, confirmation patterns, and interfaces that make the right action the easy action.

1

Plant Observation

On-site research at operating plants. Understanding workflows in the actual environment where they'll be used.

2

Compliance Mapping

Deep analysis of NRC and INPO requirements. Ensuring every design decision meets regulatory standards.

3

Error Prevention

Designing interfaces where the right action is the easy action. Making mistakes hard, not just caught.

4

Continuous Research

POD research model with 20+ presentations. Embedded partnership for ongoing improvement.

The Work

A Digital Transformation Framework

We designed multiple interconnected systems that work together to support nuclear operations, from procedure execution to work planning to AI-powered automation.

Smart Procedures

Digital procedure execution system that guides operators through complex tasks with real-time validation, automatic documentation, and error prevention at every step.

Work Planning Interface

Resource allocation and scheduling system that optimizes maintenance windows, tracks dependencies, and ensures coverage for safety-critical operations.

AI-Powered Automation

Machine learning systems that optimize workflows, predict maintenance needs, and automate routine decision-making while keeping humans in control.

Compliance Dashboard

Real-time visibility into regulatory compliance status, audit trails, and documentation requirements across all plant operations.
