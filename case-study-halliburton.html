The Challenge

When 12 Systems Become One Challenge

A global oilfield services leader needed to redesign their hydraulic fracturing data management platform. The existing interface had grown organically over years, accumulating complexity. Engineers struggled with data visualization, workflow navigation, and task completion.

Legacy Fragmentation

12 separate systems had evolved independently over decades. Each had its own interaction paradigm, data model, and user expectations.

Complex Data Visualization

Hydraulic fracturing generates massive datasets. Engineers needed to visualize, compare, and act on this data in real-time during high-stakes operations.

High-Stakes Environment

For software managing field operations, usability gaps meant efficiency gaps — and efficiency gaps meant costly delays.
