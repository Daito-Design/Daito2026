The Challenge

Illuminating the ML Black Box

3LC came to us with a powerful technology: a way to improve ML models by fixing training data in real-time. They needed help translating that technical capability into a product that data scientists would actually use and love.

Technical Complexity

The underlying technology was sophisticated: real-time data editing, model retraining, metrics tracking. Making this accessible without dumbing it down was the core challenge.

Developer Audience

Data scientists and ML engineers are demanding users. They want power and flexibility, not hand-holding. The product needed to feel like a tool for experts.

Market Positioning

The data-centric AI space was emerging but undefined. 3LC needed clear positioning that explained their unique approach: "Three Lines of Code to better models."

Our Approach

Research-Driven Product Development

We embedded with 3LC's team for 18 months, conducting deep user research, defining product strategy, and designing the end-to-end experience.

1

User Research

40+ interviews with ML engineers and data scientists across energy, finance, and healthcare sectors.

2

Market Analysis

Competitive analysis of the emerging data-centric AI landscape. Identifying white space and positioning.

3

Product Strategy

Defining the "Three Lines of Code" positioning. Developer-first tool with enterprise-grade security.

4

Design & Launch

End-to-end UX/UI design, design system, usability testing, and launch support.

The Work

A Platform Built for Data Scientists

We designed every aspect of the 3LC platform, from data visualization to sample editing to metrics dashboards, with the needs of ML practitioners at the center.

Data Visualization

Interactive 3D visualization of training data distributions, outliers, and quality issues. Making invisible data problems visible.

Sample Editing

Direct editing of training samples with real-time model impact preview. Fix data, improve models, see results immediately.

Metrics Dashboard

Track model performance improvements over time. Connect data changes to outcome improvements with clear causality.

Integration Patterns

Seamless integration with existing ML workflows. Jupyter notebooks, Python scripts, CI/CD pipelines.

Progressive Disclosure

Complex functionality revealed as needed. Power users get depth, new users get clarity.

Dark Theme UI

Navy and yellow design system optimized for long work sessions. Professional feel that respects data scientists' expertise.
