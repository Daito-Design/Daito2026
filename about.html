Enterprise software should work for the people who use it—not the other way around.

Our Story

Born from the field.

Daito Design was founded by industrial designers and human factors engineers who were frustrated with enterprise software that ignored its users.

We'd spent years working in oil fields, power plants, and manufacturing facilities—watching operators struggle with poorly designed systems. Systems built by people who'd never set foot on a platform or a factory floor.

So we started Daito Design with a simple premise: you can't design for operators if you've never watched them operate. We go to the field. We understand the work. Then we design.

Our Values

What guides us.

Field First

We don't design from conference rooms. We go where the work happens—offshore platforms, trading floors, factory lines. Understanding context is non-negotiable.

Rigor Over Trends

We apply human factors science, not design fads. Our work is grounded in research, tested with users, and built to last. Pretty isn't enough—it has to work.

Adoption Matters

A beautiful interface that nobody uses is a failure. We design for adoption, not awards—though sometimes we get both.
